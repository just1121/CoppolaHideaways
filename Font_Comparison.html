2

Headlines: Helvetica / Arial, 22px (clean sans-serif)
Body: Courier New, 14px (universal monospace)
Requires: Nothing — 100% pre-installed fonts
Works in: EVERYTHING — guaranteed on all devices
Best for: Maximum compatibility, identical everywhere
